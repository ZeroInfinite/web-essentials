How to build the menubar

The basic shapes are to have one left, one right, and then one fluid middle. Here is a simplified example:

1. Floats

.left
.right
.overflow-hidden
<div class="clearfix outline-blue mt2 mb2">
          <div class="left p2">.left</div>
          <div class="right p2">.right</div>
          <div class="overflow-hidden p2">.overflow-hidden</div>
        </div>
logo
wordmark
bell
bubble
avatar
chevron
.overflow-hidden
<div class="clearfix outline-blue mt2 mb2">
          <div class="left p1">
            <div class="left p1">logo</div>
            <div class="left p1">wordmark</div>
          </div>
          <div class="right p1">
            <div class="left p1">bell</div>
            <div class="left p1">bubble</div>
            <div class="left p1">
              <div class="left">avatar</div>
              <div class="left">chevron</div>
            </div>
          </div>
          <div class="overflow-hidden p2">.overflow-hidden</div>
        </div>

3. Make responsive

The responsive class .md-show means "only show when medium size screen or above." Go ahead and resize the window.

logo
wordmark .md-show
bell .md-show
bubble .md-show
avatar
chevron
.overflow-hidden
<div class="clearfix outline-blue mt2 mb2">
          <div class="left p1">
            <div class="left p1">logo</div>
            <div class="left p1 md-show">wordmark .md-show</div>
          </div>
          <div class="right p1">
            <div class="left p1 md-show">bell .md-show</div>
            <div class="left p1 md-show">bubble .md-show</div>
